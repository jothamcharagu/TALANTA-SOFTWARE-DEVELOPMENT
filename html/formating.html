Jotham boldly went to Mount Gerizim and shouted a famous parable to the citizens of Shechem. He comparedleadership to trees looking for a king: the valuable trees (olive, fig, vine) refused the crown to stay productive, but the worthless thornbush (representing Abimelech) greedilyx accepted it, threatening to burn down anyonewho opposed it.

He placed a prophetic curse on Abimelech and the city, predicting they would destroy each other. Jotham then fled to Beer for safety and disappeared from scriptural records. His curse was fulfilled three years later when Abimelech destroyed Shechem and was subsequently killed
